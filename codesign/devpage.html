About Me

The main developer is Camille Nica Rubia, currently a student at BYU Idaho who formerly studied Computer Secretarial years ago. She was initially inspired by her husband, a front-end web developer in creating and designing websites. She found this very interesting, how lines of code could create almost stylish and neat websites which we usually visit and use today.

As a business-minded like her, she wanted to help entrepreneurs to have their own space in the digital world to showcase their products online. Her lifetime dream is to be able to show her creativity while helping others in whatever manner she can: whether it be digitally, virtually, or physically.
